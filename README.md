# 🚀 DevConnect --  🌐 **Live Demo**: [https://dev-connect-seven-psi.vercel.app/](https://dev-connect-seven-psi.vercel.app/)  

**DevConnect** is a modern social media networking platform built for developers. It allows users to connect, share ideas, collaborate on projects, and grow as a community — all in one place.
I am building it with the vision of integrating dating feature also to tackle the loneliness of developers.



---

## ✨ Features

- 🧑‍💻 **Developer Profiles** – Showcase your skills, projects, and GitHub activity
- 💬 **Posts & Comments** – Share updates, ask questions, and discuss ideas
- 🔗 **Follow System** – Connect with other devs and build your network  - UPCOMING
- 📨 **Real-time Chat** – Chat with your connections instantly - UPCOMING
- 🌙 **Dark Theme** – Sleek and distraction-free UI
- 🤖 **AI Integration** – Smart code suggestions and post summaries - UPCOMING
- ❤️ **[Coming Soon] DevConnect Dating** – A dedicated space to match with like-minded tech enthusiasts and devs
